Urban Winery

Winery and Makerspace in Mixed Use Industrial Area

Idea

A client putting a winery in such an urban environment must be particularly interested in the properties of urbanness. This led to two major decisions in the project - one programmatic: the winery would act as a makerspace or collective for small businesses and hobbyist vintners, and one design based: the design should explore the idea of urbanness (which became density and clustering).
